Creative Flow

User Guide

What is Creative Flow?

Creative Flow is an intelligent writing assistant that helps you craft deeply personal, meaningful messages for the people you care about. It guides you through a thoughtful three-stage process to create authentic communications that truly express your feelings.

How It Works

Stage 1: Intention
Setting Your Foundation

When you start, you'll see a clean, elegant interface with a progress bar at the top showing three stages: Intention, Reflection, and Expression.

In the Intention stage, you'll fill out a simple form:

  • Recipient Name: Who are you writing to? (e.g., "Mom", "Sarah", "Alex")
  • Core Feeling: What's the main emotion you want to express? (e.g., "grateful", "proud", "loved")
  • Occasion (optional): What's the context? (Birthday, graduation, just because, etc.)
  • Tone (optional): How formal should it be? (Casual, heartfelt, formal, etc.)

Click "Begin Reflection" to move to the next stage.

Stage 2: Reflection
Gathering Your Ingredients

This is where the magic happens. The interface splits into two main areas:

Left Side - Your Ingredients Box: A large container where your thoughts and memories will be collected as you work through the prompts.

Right Side - Descriptive Words: 26 personality traits you can select that describe your recipient (Smart, Caring, Funny, Patient, etc.). Click any that feel right - they'll turn purple and get added to your ingredients.

Center - Personalized Prompts: You'll see 9 carefully crafted prompts in a 3Ã—3 grid, each designed to spark specific memories or thoughts about your recipient. These aren't generic - they're personalized based on the recipient's name and your core feeling.

Examples of prompts you might see:
  • "That time Sarah showed pure empathy"
  • "Your shared tradition of comforting gestures"
  • "When Sarah helped you feel understood"

How to Use the Prompts:

  1. Click any prompt that resonates with you
  2. A modal window opens where you can write your thoughts, memories, or feelings
  3. Your response gets added to the "Your Ingredients" box
  4. Continue with as many prompts as you want - there's no minimum or maximum

When you're satisfied with your ingredients, click "Begin Expression" to move to the final stage.

Stage 3: Expression
Crafting Your Message

The Expression stage has two equal-width boxes side by side:

Left Box - Your Ingredients: All the thoughts, memories, and descriptive words you gathered in the Reflection stage. Everything here is draggable.

Right Box - Your Message: A large text area where you'll compose your final message.

Writing Your Message:

  • Type freely in the text area
  • Drag any ingredient from the left box directly into your message
  • Use the "Weave Message" button to have the system create a complete message using all your ingredients
  • Use "Polish & Refine" to improve a message you've already written
  • The word count and estimated reading time appear below the text area

Additional Tools:

  • Copy your message to clipboard
  • Export as a text file
  • Clear the message to start over
  • Go back to add more ingredients

Key Features

Personalized Experience: Every prompt is customized based on the recipient's name and your chosen core feeling, making the suggestions highly relevant to your specific relationship.

Drag & Drop: Everything you create can be easily moved into your final message - just drag ingredients directly where you want them.

No Pressure: You can work at your own pace, skip prompts that don't resonate, and go back to any stage to add more content.

Your Voice Matters: The application assists and inspires, but your authentic voice and genuine thoughts are what make the message meaningful.

Clean, Intuitive Design: The glassmorphism interface is beautiful but never gets in the way of your creative process.

The Philosophy

Creative Flow believes that the most powerful messages come from authentic personal reflection, not from templates or generated content. The application serves as your thoughtful companion, asking the right questions and providing the right structure to help you access and organize your genuine feelings and memories.

The result is a message that truly sounds like you, feels authentic to your relationship, and expresses exactly what you wanted to say - but with more depth and consideration than you might have achieved writing from scratch.
